DSR Mobile App — User Guide

For Mentakab Pilot Champions · Daily Sales Report on your phone

App URL: https://clftrading6686-png.github.io/clf-dsr-pilot/dsr_mobile.html

Open the link on your phone's browser (Chrome / Safari). For faster access, add to home screen.

1. What is this app?

This is your digital Daily Sales Report (DSR) — it replaces the paper form you've been filling at the end of each day.

Why use it instead of paper:

  • Faster — type once, no admin re-keying into Eficore
  • Real-time — boss sees your numbers within seconds, not days
  • Per-brand — if you cover multiple brands (e.g. M-ACLO and M-MARS), record each separately in one session
  • Optional photo + GPS — proves you visited, helps everyone trust the data

The pilot runs with 5 Champions in Mentakab. Your feedback shapes the rollout to all 23 active salesmen.

3. The screen, top to bottom

3.1 Header

The dark blue bar at the top shows:

  • CLF Trading · Daily Sales Report — app name
  • Deadline countdown on the right (e.g. 5h 30m) — submit before 23:59 to avoid being flagged late

3.2 "I am" — pick your name Saya

Drop-down with the 5 Champion names. Pick yourself. Only you should select your own name — this is on your honour for the pilot.

3.3 "Brand" — pick which brand you're reporting Jenama

Drop-down with 15 Mentakab brand codes (M-ACLO, M-MARS, M-GEN, …). Defaults to your primary zone.

Important: if you covered multiple brands today, you can record them all in one session. See section 6.

These update live as you fill in customer entries.

3.5 Brand summary chips (multi-brand feature)

If you've started filling visits for more than one brand, you'll see chips like:

M-ACLO 5   M-MARS 2 · editing

Tap any chip to jump to that brand's customer list. Your work is never lost when switching.

3.6 "⭐ Monthly Priorities" card

Top 5 products the boss wants pushed this month. Hidden when boss hasn't set the list yet. When set, each customer card will let you tick which priorities you sold.

3.7 Search & Add Customer / New Outlet buttons

Two big buttons:

  • 🔍 Search & Add Customer Cari & Tambah Pelanggan — type customer name or code, pick from results
  • ➕ New Outlet Baru — for shops not yet in the master list

3.8 Today's Visits list Lawatan Hari Ini

Each customer you've added shows up here as a card. Tap to expand and fill in details.

3.9 Submit button (floating bottom)

The orange Submit button appears once you've added at least one customer. Tap to review and submit.

4. Adding a customer visit

  1. 1Tap 🔍 Search & Add Customer
  2. 2Type part of the shop's name or customer code (e.g. kedai 99 or 30M-00220)
  3. 3Pick the right one from the search results
  4. 4The customer appears in your "Today's Visits" list — tap to expand

If the shop is not in the system

Tap ➕ New Outlet, fill in the shop name + area, save. It'll be added as NEW OUTLET on this DSR.

6. Multi-brand: covering more than one brand in one session

If today you sold for two brands (e.g. M-ACLO at some shops and M-MARS at others):

  1. 1Pick your name; brand defaults to M-ACLO (your zone)
  2. 2Add the 5 customers where you sold M-ACLO; fill each in
  3. 3Now change the Brand drop-down to M-MARS
  4. 4The list switches to empty — but your M-ACLO 5 customers are still saved in memory (you'll see chip M-ACLO 5 at the top)
  5. 5Add your M-MARS customers; fill each in
  6. 6Tap Submit — the app creates 2 separate DSRs, one per brand, all at once
Don't worry about losing data — switching brands does NOT delete your earlier entries. They're held in memory until you submit.

Tap a chip like M-ACLO 5 to jump back to that brand's list and review or edit.

7. Submitting your DSR

  1. 1When done, tap the orange Submit button (bottom of screen)
  2. 2A review modal pops up showing:
    • How many DSRs you're submitting (1 per brand)
    • Per-brand breakdown: visits and sales
    • Total visits, effective calls, strike rate
    • Today's sales, collection
    • MTD vs target
  3. 3Check carefully, then tap Confirm
  4. 4Success modal shows time submitted + DSR count

Late submission

If you submit after 23:59, the system marks it LATE. The boss sees this on his dashboard. Submit on time when you can.

If submit fails

If you have no internet, the submit will fail with a red error toast. Try again when you have signal. (For now, work-in-progress is only kept in memory — don't close the browser tab until submitted.)

8. Tips for daily use

  • Add customers as you visit, not at the end of the day. Easier to remember details.
  • Capture GPS at the customer site, not afterward — the boss can see if your GPS is far from the shop.
  • Take a photo when you place a big order — proves the order is real.
  • Fill the Reason when no sale — A / B / C / D / E. The boss values this for understanding patterns.
  • Don't switch salesman drop-down during a session — that resets everything. Only pick your own name once at the start.
  • Submit by 21:00 ideally — gives boss time to review same night.
