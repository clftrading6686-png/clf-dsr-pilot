DSR Dashboard — User Guide

For the Owner / Boss · Real-time view of all 5 branches (116 active salesmen)

Dashboard URL: https://clftrading6686-png.github.io/clf-dsr-pilot/boss_dashboard.html

Best viewed on a laptop, desktop, or office TV. Works on tablets and phones too (layout stacks vertically on narrow screens).

1. What this dashboard does

This is your live window into the company Daily Sales Report operation across all 5 branches (Mentakab, Kuantan, Kemaman, Kuala Terengganu, Kota Bahru). It shows, for the selected branch and date:

  • Who submitted their DSR and what they reported
  • 4-layer Trust + Verify framework — flagging any suspicious data automatically
  • 3 visual charts — 7-day sales trend, today's leaderboard, brand mix
  • Open AI alerts — issues that need your attention
  • Live activity feed — newest visits as they happen

Data updates every 30 seconds and instantly when any salesman submits something new (real-time).

4. 🛡️ Trust + Verify · 4 Layers

This is the heart of the dashboard — your anti-cheat framework. Each layer covers one way a salesman might fake or under-report. All four turn green when clear; any non-green needs your attention.

Layer 1 — 📋 Submission Trust

Question: Did everyone submit their DSR today?

CLEARAll active salesmen submitted ≥1 brand DSR
WAITINGNo one has submitted yet (early in day)
PARTIALSome submitted, some pending — names listed below
LATEPast 23:59 deadline and missing submissions detected

Layer 2 — 📍 Location Trust

Question: Are the GPS check-ins consistent with where customers actually are?

NO DATACustomer GPS not yet in the system — variance check is dormant. Activates in Phase 2.
CLEARAll GPS check-ins within 5km of their customers
ANOMALYOne or more visits are >5km off the expected location — possible fake submission

Layer 3 — ⚖️ Sales Trust

Question: Do the salesmen's reported numbers match what Eficore actually recorded?

PENDINGEficore credentials not yet connected. Activates once IT gives us read access.
(Future)Will flag any salesman whose reported sales differ from Eficore by more than 15%

Layer 4 — 📈 Pattern Trust

Question: Is anyone's behaviour suddenly different from their 3-month pattern?

CLEARNo unusual patterns detected
ANOMALYStrike rate dropped sharply, sudden volume spike, repeated low-effort visits, etc.
How to use this: Glance at the 4 cards. Any non-green is a flag. Click into the AI Alerts panel below to see specifics, then decide if action is needed.

5. 📊 Visual Insights · 3 charts

📈 7-Day Sales Trend

Gold area chart showing total daily sales over the last 7 days ending at the date you're viewing.

  • RM 96.4K · 7-day total — sum over the 7 days
  • +18% vs prior 7d — comparison to the 7 days before that (green if up, red if down)

Use this to see momentum at a glance. A rising line week-on-week = good; a falling line = investigate.

🏆 Champion Leaderboard

Horizontal bars showing the top 8 salesmen ranked by sales on the selected day. Each bar has its own colour. Bar label shows RM amount.

  • Leader's SR code shown at top right
  • Only salesmen with sales appear; if none yet, top 5 alphabetical stubs are shown

🥧 Brand Mix Donut

Donut chart of the day's sales by brand division (M-ACLO, M-MARS, etc.). Centre shows the top brand. Each slice represents % of total sales.

What charts answer:
📈 — Is the team going up or down this week?
🏆 — Who's winning today and who needs a push?
🥧 — Is the brand mix healthy or over-concentrated?

6. 📋 Submission Status panel

For each active Mentakab salesman, shows whether they submitted today and what brands. Submitted salesmen appear first, drafts next, then pending at the bottom (scroll to see all).

  • ✓ ## Brands — green check, count of brand DSRs submitted
  • ✎ Draft — amber, started but not yet submitted
  • ◐ Pending — grey, no DSR yet

Under each name, you see:

  • Brand badges — e.g. ACLO ✓ · MARS ✓ · MDL ✎ means submitted ACLO and MARS, draft MDL
  • Total visits across all their DSRs
  • Their phone number (so you can call directly)
  • Total sales
  • Submitted time

7. 🤖 Open AI Alerts panel

Lists all unresolved alerts. Each alert shows:

  • Layer tag — L1 / L2 / L3 / L4 — which layer of Trust + Verify triggered it
  • Title + description — what happened
  • Severity — critical / high / medium / low (dot colour)
  • Time — when generated
  • Resolve button — when you've handled it, click to mark resolved

Sorted with most severe first.

How to resolve an alert

  1. Read the title + description
  2. Take action: call the salesman, ask for explanation, check Eficore, etc.
  3. Click Resolve — the alert disappears, KPI count drops

8. 🎯 Monthly Priorities Push panel

Shows the 5 products you've set as priorities for this month, with:

  • This month count — how many DSR entries pushed this product
  • +N today — how many today
  • Progress bar — visual comparison across the 5 priorities
Currently empty — please send the 5 real products and brand codes for this month. Right now the panel shows "Not configured" so salesmen aren't pushing dummy products.

9. 📍 Recent Visits Detail panel

Latest visits across the team on the selected day. Each row shows:

  • Customer name
  • Champion who visited
  • GPS check-in status (✓ if captured, "no GPS" if not)
  • GPS variance warning if >5km (Phase 2)
  • "NEW" badge for new outlets
  • Sale amount (or "No sale" if visit was unproductive)
  • Time since the visit

10. ⚡ DSR Activity Feed (bottom strip)

Horizontally scrolling cards showing the last 10 customer entries today. Each card:

  • Champion name + time ago
  • Customer name
  • Sale amount in gold
  • Tags: GPS / NEW / MISS · B (no sale with reason code)

Newest cards appear from the right with a slide animation.

11. Viewing past days (date picker)

Top right of the header has a date input.

  1. Click the date — picks any past day (cannot select future)
  2. Entire dashboard re-fetches data for that date — KPIs, charts, panels, all of them
  3. Amber Historical pill appears, so you know you're not viewing live
  4. "Today" button appears — click to jump back
Use case: "How did the team do last Saturday?" — click the date picker, pick last Saturday, the whole dashboard now shows that day.

13. Tips for daily use

  • Glance at the dashboard each morning — see what came in overnight, who's late.
  • Check the 4 Layers first — any non-green = action needed.
  • Resolve alerts daily — keeps the count low and meaningful.
  • Compare weeks via the date picker — pick last Tuesday vs this Tuesday for fair comparisons.
  • Use the leaderboard for your weekly stand-up — quick way to celebrate winners and prompt laggers.
  • Don't trust 100% — Layer 3 (Eficore) isn't on yet, so reported numbers are not yet verified. The 4 layers are designed to give you confidence, not certainty.
